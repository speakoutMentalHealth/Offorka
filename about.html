My Mission

Every meaningful conversation has the power to change a life.

I believe people rarely need someone who has all the answers. More often, they need someone willing to listen, understand and walk alongside them with wisdom, compassion and practical guidance.

My work focuses on helping individuals, leaders, schools, organizations and communities develop healthier minds, stronger resilience and more compassionate leadership.

The connection to SpeakOut

That commitment led me to establish SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach, a nonprofit organization dedicated to expanding access to mental health education, advocacy and community wellbeing across Africa.
