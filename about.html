About Jerry Nnamdi Offorka

Speaker, mental health advocate, founder, and community leader.

Who I Am

Jerry Nnamdi Offorka is a passionate mental health advocate, public speaker, and founder of SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach. His work is centered on helping people find their voice, build resilience, understand their mental wellbeing, and live with purpose.

What I Stand For

Jerry stands for healing, courage, leadership, personal growth, and community transformation. He believes that people should not suffer in silence, and that every person deserves access to support, education, encouragement, and hope.

What I Can Do

Through speaking engagements, consultations, leadership training, advocacy, and community programs, Jerry works with individuals, schools, youth groups, organizations, and communities to create healthier conversations around mental health and personal development.
