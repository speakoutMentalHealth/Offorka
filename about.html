About Jerry

More Than Mental Health.

Jerry Nnamdi Offorka is a Mental Health Consultant, Public Speaker, Leadership Development Facilitator, and Founder of SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach. His work helps people think clearly, lead confidently, and live intentionally.

Every person carries invisible battles. Some are managing pressure, some are carrying the weight of leadership, and others simply need someone who understands. Jerry's work begins where conversations become honest, calm, and practical.

Mission

To make emotional wellbeing practical and accessible.

Mental health is not only about crisis. It is about clarity, resilience, healthier relationships, better leadership, and the courage to seek support early. Jerry works with individuals, schools, organizations, faith communities, and institutions to create safe conversations that lead to action.

Healing begins when people feel seen, heard and understood.
