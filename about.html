The Story

Jerry Nnamdi Offorka is a Mental Health Consultant, Speaker, Mediator, and Founder of SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach. His work focuses on breaking the silence around mental health and helping individuals, schools, organizations, and communities access practical education, advocacy, and support.

With a background in Computer Science, NGO leadership, project management, digital strategy, mediation, and community development, Jerry combines technology, leadership, and human-centred advocacy to build healthier conversations and stronger communities.

Mission: to create a world where no one suffers in silence.
