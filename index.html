Meet Jerry

Founder, advocate, consultant, and voice for mental health transformation.

Jerry Nnamdi Ofoka leads mental health awareness and intervention work through SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach. His work focuses on breaking stigma, improving access to mental health education, strengthening institutional response, and helping people speak out before silence becomes crisis.

This personal platform serves as Jerry’s professional hub for speaking invitations, consulting engagements, books, podcast features, media appearances, and direct connection to SpeakOut programmes.
