About Jerry

A voice for mental health, leadership, and community transformation.

Jerry Nnamdi Offorka works at the intersection of mental health education, public speaking, leadership development, youth empowerment, and community impact. His work is rooted in one belief: when people are given the right language, tools, and support, healing and growth become possible.

Through speaking, consulting, digital tools, and community outreach, Jerry helps people understand emotional wellbeing, reduce stigma, build resilience, and create healthier conversations in homes, schools, workplaces, and communities.

About SpeakOut

The movement behind the mission.

SpeakOut Mental Health Outreach exists to promote mental health awareness, early intervention, youth advocacy, school-based education, community support, and digital wellbeing access. It creates safe spaces where people can speak, learn, reflect, and seek support without shame.

Mission

Accessible Mental Health Education

To make mental health education practical, stigma-free, and available to individuals, schools, communities, and organizations.

Vision

Healthier Communities

A society where people understand mental health, support one another, and seek help early before crisis becomes the only option.

Approach

Conversation First

Through public speaking, education, digital tools, resources, partnerships, and community engagement.

Areas of Work

Focused on people, impact, and practical change.

Mental Health Advocacy

Awareness, stigma reduction, early intervention, and practical emotional wellbeing education.

Leadership & Consulting

Helping leaders build emotionally intelligent teams, healthier culture, and sustainable performance.

Youth & Schools

Supporting young people with resilience, confidence, purpose, emotional literacy, and safe support pathways.
